How Cancer Spreads & Risk Factors

Genetic Factors

Some cancers can be influenced by inherited genetic mutations.

Lifestyle Choices

Smoking, alcohol, poor diet, and inactivity can increase risk.

Environmental Exposure

Pollution, chemicals, and radiation can contribute to cancer risk.

Prevention & Early Detection

Things to Avoid

  • Smoking
  • Excessive alcohol consumption
  • Exposure to carcinogens

Things to Do

  • Healthy diet & exercise
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Vaccinations where applicable
